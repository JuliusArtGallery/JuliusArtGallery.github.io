Julius Marcus Amendolia

- Born January 3, 2012 in Washington Township, NJ and moved to Florida when he was 5. -

He is now 10 years old and attends Winding Waters K-8 in Weeki Wachee, FL and is in 5th grade. He loves to draw on anything and everything, and also likes to make digital art as well on his computer. He has two older brothers who are 23 and 20 who prompted his love of video games. He also loves to watch sports on TV, and his favorites teams are the Tampa Bay Lightning and Buccaneers.
