Julius Marcus Amendolia

- Born January 3, 2012 in Washington Township, NJ and moved to Florida when he was 5. -

Julius is now 10 years old, attending the 5th grade class of Winding Waters K8 in Weeki Wachee, FL. He loves to draw on anything and everything, and even creates digital art on his computer. He has two older brothers, one 23, and the other 20, who have prompted his love for video games. He also loves to watch sports on TV, with his favorite teams being the Tampa Bay Lighting and the Buccanneers.
